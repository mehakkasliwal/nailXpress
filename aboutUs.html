About Us

Hey guys, I’m Mehak! I am currently a senior in high school and I am super excited you decided to check out nailXpress! I started this website to be able to support home-based nail artists like myself, and provide them with a platform to showcase their talent and expand their business. During this pandemic, many nail artists lost their source of income because of quarantine. So, I started this safer and permanent alternative for them to continue doing what they love and helping others with some self-care! Register with us today to get our artists’ contact information and book appointments with them :)
